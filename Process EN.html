Partnership Process / How We Work

Particle design partners with you from inside your team — from concept to implementation, all the way to lasting adoption. We put vague ideas into words and turn them into systems that work on the ground. Through the following five approaches, we bring your organization to a state where it can confidently make the next decision.

Step 01 / Strategy

Business Model & Strategy Design

We put the leader’s vague ideas and growth vision into words. By clarifying the value proposition — whose problem you solve and how — along with the revenue model, the order of validation and a mid-to-long-term roadmap, we raise the organization to a state where it can make investment decisions.

Step 02 / Workflow

Decomposing & Rebuilding Real Workflows

We visualize and break down the actual movement of people, the flow of goods and existing business processes on the ground. We identify where work depends on paper, Excel and individual know-how, and translate it into implementable requirements ready for system development.

Step 03 / Data

Building Data You Can Decide On

We organize the information lying dormant on the ground and define what data to newly capture, what to structure, and which metrics to visualize on dashboards. Rather than simply collecting data, we build a foundation that lets management and the field decide their next action — and connect it to AI.

Step 04 / Technology

Connecting Technology to Business Value

We never let technologies like RFID, NFC, QR codes, cloud and dedicated apps end at “just deployed.” We make sure they connect directly to business value — improving the real customer experience and operational efficiency — and that they take hold as lasting practice on the ground.

RFIDNFCQR codesCloudDedicated appsAI
Step 05 / PMO

Driving Projects Between Management & the Field

We stand between the concept side (management) and the implementation side (the field and development teams), acting as the command center that translates between the two. Through cross-functional coordination, governance design, PoC planning and the requirements work needed to advance to the next phase, we keep the project moving so it doesn’t stall midway.

Our Stance / What We Value

VALUE 01

A co-creation attitude

Not as an outside critic, but as a member of your team — we value partnering through problem-framing, driving, implementation and the next decision.

VALUE 02

Creating a state ready for the next decision

We don’t over-promise that we’ll “guarantee the business succeeds.” Instead, we clarify what to validate, in what order to proceed, and where the risks lie — committing to bring leaders and organizations to a state where they can confidently make the next decision.

VALUE 03

Standing with you from the concept stage

We welcome conversations even when requirements are still fuzzy and unsettled — organizing the key issues through that first dialogue and thinking through the way forward together.
